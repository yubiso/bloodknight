Blood Donation Rate on 2024

According to the Ministry of Health, Malaysia recorded 113,786 new blood donors in 2024, the second-lowest figure since 96,702 in 2021. According to the ministry's data, the number of new donors in previous years was as follows: 2023: 118,285 2022: 120,929
